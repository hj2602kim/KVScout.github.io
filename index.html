At matched storage budgets across five models (124M-14B, MHA and GQA), we find that quantization consistently outperforms rank reduction by 4-364 PPL depending on model and compression level. The gap persists even when rank reduction is combined with quantization in hybrid baselines, and it grows with GQA aggressiveness.
